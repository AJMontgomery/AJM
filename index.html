About Me

I am Ariana J. Montgomery from Decatur, Ga. I am an Interdisciplinary Studies student with an emphasis in Graphic Design, Computer Science, and Visual & Performing Arts. My diverse academic background allows me to explore the intersections of creativity, technology, and visual expression. In 2016, I founded Doc's Boutique, an online paraphernalia shop for the Marching “100,” which successfully operated for four years, selling out at pop-up events during Homecoming and Florida Classic games. During my time away from university, I launched a custom apparel business, VisionariCustomsLLC, designing and producing unique paraphernalia for current and alumni members of the Marching “100.”

Professionally, I thrive on recognizing problems and creating innovative solutions that address direct needs. My hands-on approach and creative skills have brought me success in various ventures, particularly in screen printing, vinyl cutting, and tufting. My passion for creation is matched by my love for dance and roller skating, which have led to opportunities like choreographing for the Marching “100,” auditioning for Usher and performing in film and television. I am committed to service and leadership, believing that authentic leadership is about selfless servitude, and I actively contribute to any organization or community I am part of.

Looking ahead, I aspire to make Doc's Boutique an official store for the Marching “100,” turning it into a sustainable source of revenue for scholarships, logistics, and more. Additionally, I envision becoming a brand manager for the Marching “100” to protect the organization's intellectual property better. I am also excited about possibly creating “Venom's Boutique,” a physical paraphernalia shop on campus that collaborates with student and alumni creatives to provide unique spirit wear and accessories exclusively for FAMU. This venture could be a hub for educational opportunities in apparel design and production, offering students work-study programs, scholarships, and hands-on experience.

Venom's Boutique is envisioned as a modern paraphernalia shop dedicated to serving the FAMU community. It will showcase exclusive spirit wear and accessories designed by student and alumni creatives, offering unique and innovative apparel that celebrates our rich heritage. This boutique aims to provide educational opportunities in design and production, fostering creativity and skill-building.

Venom's Boutique Executive Summary

Mission

  • Create a modern paraphernalia shop that caters to the FAMU community.
  • Collaborate with student and alumni designers to produce exclusive apparel and accessories.
  • Provide educational opportunities for students interested in apparel design and production.

Vision

  • Establish Venom's Boutique as a physical store on FAMU's campus.
  • Foster a community-focused brand that represents FAMU pride and spirit.
  • Offer students hands-on work-study opportunities and scholarships.

Key Products

  • Custom FAMU apparel, including exclusive designs from students and alumni.
  • Spirit wear, accessories, and merchandise to promote FAMU pride.

Educational Opportunities

  • Provide mentorship and skill development for students in apparel design and production.
  • Potential work-study programs that teach hands-on business and design experience.
  • Collaborate with university departments to integrate learning and entrepreneurial opportunities.

Long-Term Goals

  • Transform Venom's Boutique into a sustainable revenue-generating hub for the FAMU community.
  • Support scholarships and student programs through boutique profits.
  • Protect the intellectual property and branding of FAMU through official merchandise.
